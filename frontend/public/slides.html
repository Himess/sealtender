TÜRKIYE
KIK 4734 sayılı Kanun Madde 36 says bidder names are "only known by the procurement commission." Commission staff are human. Leaks recur in audit reports. The legal property has never been enforced cryptographically.
GLOBAL
World Bank and OECD anti-bribery surveys repeatedly cite bid-rigging in 15–20% of audited public-procurement files across developing economies, with cartels typically inflating winning prices 15–30%.
IMPACT
When over-charging is structural, what gets cut is concrete, rebar, oversight. Bid-rigging is not abstract — it shows up in the physical world long after the tender closes.
This is not a software bug. It is a 200-year structural assumption that humans in the middle won't peek. They do.
THE PARADOX

Open tenders leak prices. Sealed tenders trust the operator.

Cryptography has tried to fix this. Commit-reveal needs a reveal phase that can stall. MPC needs every bidder online for the ceremony. TEE needs hardware vendors you have to trust. Each solution adds a new trust point.
FHE is the first PRACTICAL primitive that gives privacy AND transparency together.

THE FHE PRIMITIVE · TWO ENCRYPTED RAILS, ONE TENDER

Compute on ciphertext. Decrypt only the result. With a quorum.

Price, years, projects encrypted client-side; compared blind on-chain.

BOND ENVELOPE · ERC-7984 cUSDC
operator → confidentialTransferFrom → escrow holds euint64
ACL: FHE.allowThis + FHE.allow(bidder)
ENCRYPT CLIENT-SIDE
FHE ciphertext for price/years/projects/bond. Bound to (contract, user) by an input ZK proof — Zama's input verification gates ciphertext authenticity before FHE compute begins.
EVALUATE ON-CHAIN
FHE.lt + FHE.select find the minimum without ever seeing plaintext. ~700K gas per submitBid.
DECRYPT WITH QUORUM
9-of-13 KMS threshold (Zama TKMS, mainnet live since Dec 30, 2025). FHE.checkSignatures binds cleartext on-chain.

TRUST MODEL · 5 PILLARS

Each property is enforced cryptographically — and we're explicit about residual trust.

1
PRIVACY · BID VALUES STAY SEALED
Enforced by 9-of-13 KMS threshold · Residual trust: 5 colluding operators not enough.
2
INTEGRITY · NO FABRICATED CLEARTEXT
Enforced by FHE.checkSignatures binds plaintext to KMS attestation · Residual trust: we don't believe a fabricated cleartext.
3
LIVENESS · NO SINGLE OPERATOR CAN STALL
Enforced by permissionless reveal pipeline + forceCancelStuckReveal · Residual trust: none.
4
DISPUTES · COMMISSION, NOT OWNER
Enforced by 3-of-5 ArbitrationSafe (KIK + Idari Mahkeme + 2 sector reps + 1 NGO) · Residual trust: 4734 Madde 12 commission rule on chain.
5
LIQUIDITY · DEPOSITS NEVER FROZEN
Enforced by BidEscrow.claimRefund permissionless after Cancelled · Residual trust: none.
